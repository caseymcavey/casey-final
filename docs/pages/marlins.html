Miami Marlins

NL East

The Miami Marlins are an American professional baseball team based in Miami. The Marlins compete in Major League Baseball (MLB) as a member club of the National League (NL) East division. The franchise began play as an expansion team in the 1993 season as the Florida Marlins. As part of a stadium agreement with Miami-Dade County, the franchise also changed its name to the Miami Marlins prior to the 2012 season. The Marlins are the first team to win the World Series as a wild card.
